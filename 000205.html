From the way-back machine

While we were hunting around for a floppy disk that Carolyn could use to bring some files to school, we stumbled across this picture:







Optimizing DCOM class, DevelopMentor August 22, 1997



This was the class picture taken at the end of the Optimizing DCOM class that I took from Don Box in preparation for becoming a COM instructor at DevelopMentor. It was taken outside of DM's old office on Voyager Blvd in Torrance, CA.


I'm pretty sure that this photo was taken using Don's DSC F2 digital camera - a 640 x 480 marvel of technology in its day. I met Don at the inaugural design review for the CLR (called COR back then), in Redmond on June 25, 1997 when Charlie Kindel introduced us. Man, it seems like just yesterday. How time flies!
