Practical Eye for the .NET Guy

The second issue of my newsletter, Practical Eye for the .NET Guy is now out.

I'd like to thank my illustrious panel of reviewers for making sure that the number of errors in my newsletter were kept to a minimum: Alex Kipman and Dan Moseley of the MSBuild team for their patience with my numerous questions [and complaints :)], Steve Loughran and Stefan Bodewig of the Ant team for continuing to share with me their experiences with complex builds in the Java and .NET worlds, Ian MacLean of the NAnt team for reviewing the newsletter with an eye on how it compares to NAnt, John Robbins from Wintellect for enlightening on the finer details of PDB file resolution, and Jean Rajotte of Frogware for his helpful critical comments about my style.

Any errors, of course, are still my fault. Enjoy!
