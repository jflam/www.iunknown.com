My Plan

So, what am I up to these days?

I spend some of my time thinking about Microsoft's Common Language Runtime.

I am spending an increasing amount of time thinking about Generative Programming.

I still think about Aspect-Oriented Programming, although I'm not as convinced of its ability to change the world as I once was.

Now that the economy seems to be on the road to recovery, I actually am teaching once a month for Wintellect.

I spend quite a bit of time pondering the effectiveness of instructor-led training. I am working on some technology that should demonstrably improve the ability of my students to recall what I taught them when it comes time for them to actually use that knowledge.

I am the C++ and C# conference chair for the following DevConnections conferences in 2003:

  • New Orleans, May 6-9, 2003
  • Palm Springs, Oct 12(?), 2003

I also plan on speaking at the following conferences in 2003:

  • WinDev in Boston sometime in the Spring of 2003
  • WinSummit in Davos, CH, October, 2003

I plan on writing various rants here about technology from time to time.
