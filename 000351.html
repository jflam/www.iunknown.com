Having reviewed extensive research into the origins of Murphy's Law, and the apparent intent of the person that coined the original phrase, I suggest that this statement is best characterized as "Murphy's Nerd Cousin's Law". It is a lot less general than Murphy's Law.

Then there's Murphy's Anal Retentive Cousin's Law: A minor adjustment to a perfect system will send it crashing down like a house of cards.
