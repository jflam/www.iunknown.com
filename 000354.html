Java Development with Ant

Steve Loughran and Erik Hatcher wrote what I consider to be the definitive guide to Ant. This is a book that is designed to be definitive, and is full of technical details about writing effective Ant scripts. The authors spend a fair amount of time discussing deployment issues as well. Most other books treat Ant as a build tool, which it is, but automating the deployment process is arguably just as important as building your application. There are also a number of excellent stories in this book about the authors' real-world experience in using Ant in production environments. Highly recommended.
