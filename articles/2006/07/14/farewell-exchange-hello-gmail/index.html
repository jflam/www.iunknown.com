Farewell Exchange, Hello Gmail

Posted by John Lam Sat, 15 Jul 2006 03:36:00 GMT

You were a pretty good friend for many years.

For as long as iunknown.com has existed, I’ve been running my own Exchange Server. Based on looking at my DNS settings, That’s been nearly 9 years! But now, the spam problem has gotten so bad that I’m pretty much resigned to giving up on running my own mail server.

I’m now running all of my domain-based (jlam at this domain) email through Google’s new GMail on your domain service. It’s free, I’ve got 2+GB of storage, and I’ve got integration with my Mac’s Mail application.

So far I’m very happy with this new solution.
