Misconceptions on Pollution and causes of Cancer

During graduate school in the early 90's, I spent a lot of time reading scientific literature. One paper that has strongly influenced me since those days was Bruce Ames' paper on Misconceptions on pollution and the causes of Cancer. If you are curious at all about the impact of pesticides and pollution on the food that we eat, you should definitely read this paper (and be prepared to re-examine your assumptions).

Unfortunately, you will most likely have to visit your local university library to gain access to the article.

Update: Thanks to David Stone for finding an updated version of the paper here: http://www.ncpa.org/studies/s214.html.
