Lawrence Lessig on Free Culture

This is one of the most creative presentations that I have ever seen. Pedagogically, this "slide deck" (really it's a Flash movie) rocks. His "refrain" is the central point of the talk and he revisits it after making each new point. It's much better than the standard way of presenting your "agenda" on a slide that's used to separate sections of your talk. Each time he revisits his refrain, he paraphrases it in a different way.
