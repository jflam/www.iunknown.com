Backstage at MIX


Blast off!, originally uploaded by John Lam.

I was backstage all day Sunday from about 10am to 11pm getting things setup for Scott's demo of the DLR and IronRuby during his keynote. This was the first time that I've been involved in a keynote; it's absolutely amazing how much equipment was involved in pulling off the show.

I only brought my 80-200 f/2.8 lens to the show, so I couldn't capture the ideal wide-angle shot. This was just one of the many, many consoles that folks were using to control ... stuff.
