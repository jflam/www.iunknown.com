Brent Rector rocks!

Hopefully all of you who are attending the PDC are now holding a copy of Brent Rector's Developers' Guide to Longhorn in your hands. Brent was kind enough to send me a soft copy of his book, so I got to read some fun stuff from our old Essential COM days back at DevelopMentor that didn't make it past the editors (they just don't get it, do they, Brent?)

This is an excellent book. Brent delivers a very clear and organized introduction to the vast API space of Longhorn. There's detailed coverage of XAML, MSBuild, WinFS, data binding, Indigo. Hopefully MS Press will fast-track this book so that folks who couldn't make it to the PDC can get up to speed quickly on Longhorn.
