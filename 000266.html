About this site

This web log is a place where I write my observations on various software development topics. For example, last year I wrote quite a bit about my runtime aspect weaver for the CLR. Weblogs are also interesting mediums for carrying on public on-line conversations with other folks. See my links for other folks whose weblogs I actively monitor using Aggie. If you're interested in who I am and what I do, look here.

What's up with this domain name? Well, back in 1997, during my first visit to DevelopMentor prior to my becoming an instructor there, Don Box and I drove over to the DMV to pick up his new vanity plate for his car. It was, of course, IUNKNWN (because California, unlike Ontario, Canada where I live only lets you have 7 letters on your vanity plates). Now wanting to be outdone, when I flew home, I checked to see if this domain was taken. The rest, of course, is history.
