Practical Eye for the .NET Guy

I'm happy to announce the publication of the first issue of my newsletter, Practical Eye for the .NET Guy. The newsletter will cover practical issues that every .NET developer is bound to run across.

In the inaugural issue of Practical Eye for the .NET Guy, find out why a week with a group of Programmable Logic Controller developers changed the way I think about the importance of build scripts in your software. You will also find a Practical Developers' Guide to NAnt, which will get you writing your first NAnt build scripts within 30 minutes or your money back!

You can subscribe to Practical Eye for the .NET Guy using the sidebar on my weblog at http://www.iunknown.com.
