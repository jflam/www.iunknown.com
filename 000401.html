Spit n' polish in ASP.NET 2.0

I've spent the better part of the day looking closer at ASP.NET 2.0. I'm really impressed by the amount of polish and attention to detail in the product. Here are a couple of features that represent some nice examples of "polish" - they aren't all that hard to write yourself, but it sure was nice that somebody else wrote them for you already:

  • URL mapping: there's a new section in web.config called <urlMappings> which let you map old URLs to new URLs.
  • Image generation service: there's a new type: System.Web.UI.Image.ImageGenerator that lets you write to a Graphics object that it creates on the fly for you by overriding its RenderImage method.
