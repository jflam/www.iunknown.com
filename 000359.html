Extreme Programming with Ant

This is a book with a story to tell, and it does a great job telling it. It introduces Ant and a whole suite of open-source tools: XDoclet, JUnit, JContract, Jalopy among others. It follows the story of a fictional company called Networks Byte Design and its team of developers as they incrementally build their own software assembly line. Each section of the book introduces the problem in the form of a story, and then proceeds to explain how to solve it using the tools that are introduced in that section. Highly recommended for anyone who is interested in software assembly lines in general, and not just for those who are implementing them in Java.
