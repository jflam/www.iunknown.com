Two and a half weeks

Wow. I can't believe that it's been two and a half weeks since Matthew arrived. Here are a few observations from a new dad:

  1. He's really alert for a few minutes at a time. I've seen him studying my glasses when I've been holding him up near my face.
  2. Baby Bjorn rules! A friend gave us her Baby Bjorn baby carrier (thanks, Nat!) and I've been using it a lot recently. It settles him down very quickly, and it lets me get a lot of household stuff done as well.
  3. Baby congestion really sucks. We've had to use saline + snot sucker a few times to clear things up. Having him sleep in his car seat also helps as well.
  4. When your son is breastfeeding son, it's hard to feel particularly useful. I've dealt with this problem by assuming the domestic duties + hunter / gatherer role. I regularly go out and restock various supplies that baby and parents need to continue to function.

Thanks again to everyone who wrote in. Quite a lot of the tips came in very useful in the first few weeks. I kid you not when I say that we got over 20 pages of advice from readers of my blog! You guys (and gals) rule!
