Victoria College

2013-08-10

We wanted to show Ben and Matthew where Mom and Dad met and went to school

This was the stairway do Ned's Cafe where many of our pub nights were held. It's nice to see that they preserved the stairway during the massive remodeling of Wymilwood.

This is what Ned's Cafe looks like today from the entrance:

This is what the outside of Wymilwood looks like today. There's a brand new addition on the right hand side of the building:

This is what Wymilwood used to look like in 1964:

Here, Ben is modeling in front of the Vic Field. Behind him is the McKinsey building. That used to be a parking lot that was adjacent to the athletic field. Behind there used to be a field house, but that parcel of land was sold off quite a while ago to finance ... ?

Matthew is standing in front of the entrance to Burwash hall, which is where we ate all of our meals. This was a fantastic place to both eat and just hang out in once meals were over.

Here, you get a sense of just how airy our dining hall is. Most of what you see here (with the exception of the salad bar in the foreground) is the same as it was when we were there. The photo was shot from the high table which sits on a slightly raised platform at one end of the hall. Originally, each of the tables was assigned to one of the 9 houses (North, Gate, Middle, South, Ryerson, Nelles, Caven, Bowles & Gandier). When we made the dining hall co-ed, additional tables were added to accomodate the 6 floors of Margaret Addison and Annseley.

Here, you see the Old Vic Building. I love how giant the tree is in the quad.

Here's a view from Pratt Library looking at the Lower Houses with Matthew in a tree.

Here's a close-up shot of Old Vic with Ben posing on some new sculpture thing that was added.

Robots?! We didn't have no stinking robots in our time. Though it would be have been cool to take this class!
