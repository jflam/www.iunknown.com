What's the big deal about Safari?

Is it just me, or is Safari a solution looking for a problem? I've run this browser for a few days on my Mac, and I personally don't see any difference in rendering speed when doing side-by-side comparisons with IE. Even if it were faster, I suspect that most of speed difference would be soaked up by network latency.


Personally, I look at web browsing as a solved problem. There are far more important and interesting problems to spend engineering dollars on than building yet another web browser.
