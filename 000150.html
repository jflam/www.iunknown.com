Great injet printer review site

Over on DPReview, there were some folks who posted this item in their forums about their new inkjet printer review site. This site is extremely well done, and a must-read for folks contemplating a new inkjet printer purchase.


I've been very happy with my rather old Epson Stylus 860. In side-by-side comparison prints with a calibrated Canon S900, I felt that my printer held up quite well, considering that it only uses a 4 color (CMYK) ink system. After reading the reviews, I'm seriously considering purchasing a Canon S9000 so that I can print 13" wide prints at home.
