I have to give Virtual PC 2004 a go

I had a fun time last week teaching at HP in Fort Collins. While the class was smaller than normal, it gave us time to explore a lot of interesting ideas about how to build better software, faster.

On Friday night, I had dinner in Denver with a number of local blogger / celebrity types. Brad Wilson, Greg Reinacker, Gordon Weakliem, Keith Brown, and Peter Provost all in attendance. We had a lot of fun talking geek late into the night. A special thanks goes out to Brad Wilson for pulling this one together.

One of the really interesting things that I learned was just how good Virtual PC 2004 was. I'm currently a VMWare 4.0 Workstation user, but I'm going to spend a bit of time running VPC 2004 to see just how well it stacks up against VMWare.

Update: Does anyone know if VPC 2004 runs OK on Win2K3 Server? The installer claims that it's an unsupported OS, but the installer still lets you go ahead.
